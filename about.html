About Us

EDC, JSSATE Noida aims to induce an entrepreneurial mindset into the students and air an innovative streak in them. We are here to water the ‘Ideas’ in the bud and help them bloom into impactful endeavors through networking student enterprises from campus to incubators, seeding funds and angel investors to transform the newly proposed ideas into successful start-ups.
ECell JSSATE Noida is a consortium of imagineers working towards manifesting the latent entrepreneurial spirit of young curious students. Since its inception in 2015 by some aspiring minds like ours, EDC is committed to working in the direction to foster entrepreneurship.

Vision

  • Provide centerstage to business ideas out of which the most influential ideas are guided and funded by the corporate leaders.
  • Organize events and interactive sessions to educate the student aspirants about the real corporate ecosystem.
  • Dynamically develop leadership qualities and entrepreneurship skills and qualities among the young minds
  • Work with some of the promising startups and collaborate with them on all possible fronts.
  • Focus on prioritizing the “out of the box “ thinking among the students and encourage them to find innovative and rational approaches to their problems.

Mission

We aim to arouse and encourage the spirit of entrepreneurship in young blooming minds and help them cultivate this zeal by providing them with various opportunities and interactive sessions along with certain challenges and competitions. We seek to provide a resourceful and effective platform to varnish their business knowledge, and hone leadership skills and team spirit in these young aspirants, so they transform into independent, resilient, socially sensitive learners for life.
