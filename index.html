Frequently asked questions

Startups need to register for the event through the form and submit their pitch deck. Startups must get the investor pitch access pass after registration.
Startups will pitch to a panel of investors through the medium of a virtual meeting room. On completion of the pitch, the individual pitching deck of all the shortlisted startups will be made available to all the investors.
The registered and interested participants will be submitting their CVs through a form. Next, the CVs will be made available to the startups. The startups will be contacting the shortlisted candidates for their interviews through a suitable medium.
Owing to the pandemic, the Internship Fair will prove to be fruitful for startups as the students are continuously honing their skills while in lockdown. Also due to the decline in opportunities owing to pandemic again, students will get the right opportunity to gain some professional experience.
Registered attendees will be provided login details through which they will be able to join the session. Once the session begins, the expert will teach the participants for about 2 hours. In the end, the participants will have a skill and it’ll be certified by the expert itself.
The participant must be registered for the E-Summit-2020. The login details will be provided by the organizers through which they will be able to join the sessions. The speakers will walk the attendees through a crucial part of a startup life cycle which includes:
Ideation
